AASB S2 §24 Guidance

❌ Not yet started — This section is mandatory and must be completed before the Risk Management Disclosure can be finalised.
§23 — Risk & Opportunity Processes
An entity shall disclose information about its processes for identifying, assessing, and managing climate-related risks and opportunities and whether those processes are integrated into its overall risk management framework.
Mandatory
§24 — Prioritisation
An entity shall disclose information about its processes for prioritising climate-related risks and opportunities, including how those processes are integrated into the entity's overall risk management. Disclose: (a) identification inputs; (b) assessment criteria; (c) prioritisation method; (d) ERM integration status.
Mandatory
§25 — Monitoring
An entity shall disclose information about how it monitors climate-related risks and opportunities. This includes monitoring frequency, governance oversight, and how the entity responds to changes in the risk landscape over time.
Mandatory
§24(d) — ERM Integration
Disclose whether and how the entity's processes for managing climate-related risks are integrated into its overall risk management. If not integrated, provide context on current arrangements and any planned integration steps.
Mandatory
