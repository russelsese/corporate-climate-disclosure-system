AASB S2 Quick Reference

§6 — Governance Body
Identify the governance body or bodies (e.g. board, committee) responsible for oversight of climate-related risks and opportunities.
Mandatory
§7(a) — Board Oversight
Disclose how the governance body oversees climate-related risks and opportunities, including how it is informed and how it integrates climate into strategy and risk.
Mandatory
§7(b) — Management Role
Disclose management's role in overseeing climate-related risks and opportunities, including relevant controls and procedures.
Mandatory
§8 — Remuneration
Whether and how executive remuneration policies are linked to climate-related considerations, or explain why remuneration is not linked.
Mandatory
§9 — Transition Plan Link
Reference to transition plan governance arrangements where relevant to board oversight disclosures.
Conditional
Mandatory from: 1 July 2026 (Group 1 entities). Ensure all governance disclosures are finalised before annual report lodgement.
