Disclosure requirement: If executive remuneration is not linked to climate metrics, entities must disclose this fact and explain why it is not applicable, or document the board's rationale. This applies to all Key Management Personnel (KMP) as defined under AASB 124.

AASB S2 §8 Guidance

§8 — Remuneration
Whether and how climate-related considerations are factored into executive remuneration policies and decisions during the reporting period.
Mandatory
Specifically disclose:
  • Whether remuneration policies link pay outcomes to the management of climate-related risks and the achievement of climate-related targets or milestones
  • The percentage of total remuneration linked to climate metrics (by role)
  • If no linkage exists — why not, or a statement that it is not applicable to the entity
KMP Scope
Disclosure applies to Key Management Personnel (KMP) as defined in AASB 124 Related Party Disclosures, including executive directors and senior management with authority over the entity's direction.
No linkage — rationale
Where remuneration is not linked, a clear statement of rationale is required. Simply stating "not applicable" without explanation is insufficient for AASB S2 compliance.
