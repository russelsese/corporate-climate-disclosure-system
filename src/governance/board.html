AASB S2 §7(a) Guidance

§7(a) — Oversight Requirements
Disclose information about the governance body or bodies' oversight of climate-related risks and opportunities, including:
  • How the body is informed about climate-related risks and opportunities
  • How the body considers climate when overseeing strategy, major plans of action, risk policies and performance objectives
  • How the body oversees the setting of targets related to climate and monitors progress
  • Whether dedicated competencies or skills exist for climate oversight
Evidence Required
Board or committee minutes demonstrating climate as a standing agenda item. Charter documentation of climate mandate.
Mandatory
Frequency
At minimum, evidence of climate oversight at each quarterly board or committee cycle during the reporting period.
✓ Section complete

4 oversight activities documented covering the full FY2025 reporting period.
