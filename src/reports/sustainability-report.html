1. Governance

The Board of AusMetal Holdings Pty Ltd maintains oversight of climate-related risks and opportunities through its dedicated Sustainability Committee. The Committee meets quarterly and receives climate risk updates at each meeting. The Board has approved the company's net-zero 2045 commitment and oversees progress toward interim targets.

Management responsibility for climate matters is delegated to the Chief Financial Officer (Alex Chen), supported by the ESG Manager (Sarah Mitchell). The CFO holds accountability for climate-related financial disclosures and reports to the Board at each quarterly meeting.
