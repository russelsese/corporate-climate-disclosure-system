AASB S2 — Strategy Disclosure Requirements Action Required

Climate-related risks & opportunities (§10–§12)

Describe the climate-related risks and opportunities that could reasonably be expected to affect the entity's prospects, including physical and transition risks.

Scenario analysis (§13–§14)

Entities must use climate-related scenario analysis to assess climate resilience. A minimum of two scenarios are required — including one consistent with 2°C or lower warming.

Transition plan (§15–§20)

Where a transition plan exists, entities must disclose it, including targets, capital allocation strategies, and dependencies on government policy or technology.

Business model & value chain (§21–§22)

Disclose the current and anticipated effects of climate-related risks and opportunities on the entity's business model and value chain, including strategic decisions made.
