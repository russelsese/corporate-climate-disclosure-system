AASB S2 Guidance

§13–§14 requirements

§13

An entity shall use climate-related scenario analysis to assess its climate resilience. The analysis must reflect the entity's specific circumstances and exposures.

§14(a)

At least two climate scenarios must be used. One must be consistent with limiting global warming to 1.5°C or 2°C. This is mandatory.

§14(b)

Scenarios must be relevant to the entity's circumstances and consider a range of plausible climate futures, including where possible those that pose the greatest risk.

§14(c)

Disclose the time horizons used. Where the entity does not have the capability to conduct robust scenario analysis, it must explain its approach and planned improvements.

Modified Liability

Under Corporations Act s1013B, forward-looking statements in scenario analysis attract modified liability protection for FY2025–FY2027 disclosures.
